These cars all make me feel something that other cars just don't. There is no rhyme or reason to it, it's just the way it is. Here's the list in no particular order...

- Subaru WRX STI Gen 2 (either iteration, this is nostalgic from when I was in high school. Blue with Gold wheels is the only way)
- Mercedes-Benz SLR McLaren (pure car porn)
- Lamborghini Huracan Sterrato (this makes me want to do Mad Max things)
- Lexus LFA (holy fuck, this might be the best car ever made IMO)
- Audi R8 RWS (something about the simplicity and daily usability entices me)
- Porsche 911 GT3 Touring Manual (a GT3 for daily use? Sign me up! In Oak Green Metallic Neo)
- McLaren F1 (I'll probably never get to own one of these)
